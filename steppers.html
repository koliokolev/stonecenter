Flagstone Steppers




Flagstone steppers are natural stone in irregular shapes up to 24" in diameter. This material can be used for walks, patios, ponds and stepping stone. Below are some of the Steppers that are available. Come in to see the full selection of colorful flagstones and steppers.



Hickory Steppers

A natural cleft surface. Approx. 1" in thickness. Color: gray, dark gray to charcoal. Sizes varied.



Semco Steppers

A natural cleft surface. Approx. 1" to 2" in thickness. Color: cream, orange, brown to buff. Sizes varied.



Misty Rose Steppers

A natural cleft smooth surface. Approx. 1" to 2" in thickness. Color: peach, pink, white to buff. Sizes varied.



Fond du Lac Steppers

A smoother natural cleft surface. Approx. 1" to 2" in thickness. Color: gray, blue gray to buff. Sizes varied, large or smaller.



Wisconsin Steppers

A dimpled surface. Approx. 1" to 2" in thickness. Color: gray to buff. Sizes varied.



New York Bluestone

A smooth natural cleft surface. Approx. 1/2" to 1" in thickness. Color: green, blue, brown, rust to purple. Sizes varied.



Sandy Oak Creek Steppers

A smooth natural cleft surface. Approx. 2" to 3" in thickness. Color: cream, orange, brown to buff. Sizes varied



Imported Porphyry Steppers

A smooth surface stepper. Approx. 1" in thickness. Color: four colors. Sizes varied.
