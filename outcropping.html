Outcropping




Large pieces of limestone or sandstone imbedded in hillsides or strategically placed within the landscaping is referred to as outcropping. Used to give a rustic and natural look, it mimics the stone bluffs and escarpments found in nature. Available in a variety of sizes, colors, and types of stone it is ideal as accents in planting beds, natural retaining walls and even rustic steps or stairways.
